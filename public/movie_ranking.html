The best Movies according to Pieter

My favourite movies of all time.

Animation

Berzerk

This one is hard to explain. The art style is unique and the story is gripping. Not for everyone as it can be a lot to take in at times, some scenes are explisit and tragic but this just builds and adds to the complexity of Guts's character.

Gost in the Shell

Drama

The Jacket

I love this movie due to the emotions and its ability to make you feel for the character. It also makes you think about reality. The musical score is also haunting. It just seems to have stuck with me

Green Street Hooligans

This was a favourite in my younger days. It made me also want to become more self sufficient, grow with close friends, and become my own person, doing stuff for me.

Adventure

Into the Wild

This movie is great, it's a coming of age film, and the thoughts about the meaning of modern day life and just going on an adventure are unique. Its sad in the end but it makes you think on your own life and if you have even lived it.

Sci Fi

Bladerunner

Ex Machina
